Agiliti Youth Association

The youth association primary aim is to serve as a platform for youths in the community to air their views and to be involved in stragedy formulation and decision making towards development of the the community.

Cancellation of meetings

All CDA and youth meetings are suspended till the state lockdown is lifted. We hereby advice all community members to adhere to the federal government order of staying indoors.

Late hour movement restriction

The community gate will be closed by 11:30pm and opened by 04:30am. Any movement between the restricted hours will be prosecuted.

General CDA meetings

General CDA meeting holds last saturdays of the month at the community health center building, Idi-Oparun.

General Youth meetings

The Youth general meeting holds every first and last sundays of the month at the community health center building, Idi-Oparun.
